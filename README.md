## Research question: "Ever told you that you have a form of depression?"

- Data comes from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
- Data collected from all 48 states.
- "Ever told you that you have a form of depression?". The program what percent of people said "yes" to this question by state.
- The CSV (program2.csv) shows year, state, state_abbr, percent_yes (percent of people who answered yes to that they have depression)
